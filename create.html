Help


Don't have an Identification String?

The Treehouse care package system is a tool used by DSHS employees and Treehouse volunteers only. To receive a Identification String, please email example@treehouse.com.


Forgot your Identification String or password?

If you have forgotten your Identification String or password, please email example@treehouse.com receive your identification string and reset your password.



Changing your password

If you would like to change your password, please email example@treehouse.com with your identification string and credentials.


This website is not associated with Treehouse or DSHS.
